How to Build A "Usable" PC

2019-06-20

Choice of Hardware

Here is my hardware list:

CPU: i5-9400f 
GPU: MSI RTX VENTUS 2060 
Motherboard: MSI B360M
Memory: DDR4 2133MHZ 16GB * 1 
Storage:Samsung SSD 860 EVO 250GB + Intel 760P NVMe M.2 256G + Seagate 2T 3.5 HDD

How to choose the hardware? In my opinion, you could just google the keywords, for intance, “Deep Learning PC set-up”, “Electronic Engineering PC set-up” and you can find the those pretty good set-up list. If you are a computer idiot, you can just follow the list, or you can just customize a list based on the “template” (I called those lists “template”)

Why I choose this set-up

Basically, this set-up is for my final year project (FYP) and the most vital point is that the graphic card: I need to use the CUDA to do the parallel computation which means I need a NVIDIA’s card. and at that time (June, 2019), the RTX super is not on sale, I chose RTX 2060 instead. I don’t need a powerful card like RTX 2080 Ti since I can access to the deparment server and it provides me a NVIDIA TESLA P100.

Also, RTX 2060 can runs most of the games in Steam, Blizzard or Epic. (May be the Gamming is the main reason to buy this PC)

Storage is inportant, SSD is neccessary these days, and HDD can provides a large storage to store my datas and caches (and games).
